Edo-Nigeria

Edo State is a state located in the southern part of Nigeria. It is most famous for being home to the great Benin kingdom, the home of the binis or the Edo people as they are locally called. The Modern day Benin kingdom is most renowed for its monarcy system with roots dating as far back as 1400. The Ancient Benin kingdom was among the first precolonial West Africa kingdoms to established trade route with the Europeans.
